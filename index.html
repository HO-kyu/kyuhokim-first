About This Tool

Have you ever wondered if you have the playful energy of a dog or the calm demeanor of a cat? Our "Pet-Alike" tool uses the power of machine learning to analyze your facial features and determine your animal look-alike!

This fun experiment is built using a technology called Teachable Machine by Google. We trained a computer model by showing it thousands of images of dogs and cats. The model learned to recognize the distinct patterns, shapes, and features of each animal. When you show it your face, it makes its best guess based on the patterns it learned. It's all done in your browser, and we never see or save your images.

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is critically important to us. This policy outlines how we handle your data.

Image Processing: All image analysis, whether from your webcam or an uploaded file, happens entirely on your device within your web browser. Your images are never sent to our servers, and they are not stored or saved by us in any way.

Webcam Access: Your web browser will ask for your permission before this site can access your webcam. Access is only used for the duration of the real-time analysis and is turned off immediately when you switch modes or leave the page.

Contact Form: If you use our contact form, the information you provide (email and message) will be sent to us via the Formspree service so we can respond to your inquiry. Please do not send sensitive information through this form.
